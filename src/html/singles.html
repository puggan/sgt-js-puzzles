Rules

  • The same number may only appear once per row and column.
  • Removed/Blocked numbers can't be neighbor with other Removed/Blocked numbers (N,W,S,E).
  • All numbers most be connected with the rest of the numbers (N,W,S,E).

How to Play

  • Remove number by tapping them with the remover-tool. (default left mouse-button).
  • Mark numbers as required by tapping them with the keep-tool. (default right mouse-button, or 2 left).
