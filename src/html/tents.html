Rules

  • Each Tree is paired/connected to one adjacent Tent. (4 dir)
  • Each Tent is paired/connected to one adjacent Tree. (4 dir)
  • No Tent is adjacent to another Tree. (8 dir)
  • A Row/Column should have exactly the number of Tent as shown by the number.

How to Play

  • Place Tents adjacent to Trees (default left mouse-button).
  • Mark space an Non-Tent (default right mouse-button, or 2 left).
  • Mark None-Pair (default left mouse-button on a boarder).
